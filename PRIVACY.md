# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Keiro ("we", "us", the "App") collects, uses,
and protects information when you use the App. By using Keiro you agree to the
practices described here.

> **Note:** This is a starter policy provided as a template. It is not legal
> advice. Have it reviewed by a qualified professional before relying on it for
> a commercial product.

## 1. Who we are

Keiro is a mobile-first invoicing application for delivery drivers and small
businesses. The App lets you create invoices, track payments, and manage store
and product information.

If you have questions about this policy, contact: **[your-email@example.com]**

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect the information needed to provide the App's features:

- **Account information** — the email address you use to sign in, managed
  through our authentication provider (Supabase). Passwords are never stored by
  us in readable form; authentication is handled by Supabase.
- **Business and invoice data you enter** — your business name and phone,
  invoice details (numbers, dates, line items, totals), customer/store names,
  store phone numbers and addresses, payment records, notes, and signatures you
  add to invoices.
- **Device-local preferences** — settings such as theme, display density, and
  onboarding status, stored locally on your device.

We do **not** collect payment card numbers, bank details, or government IDs.
The App does not process payments.

## 3. How your data is stored

- Data is stored in your account in **Supabase** (our cloud database provider)
  and protected by per-user **Row Level Security**, so each account can only
  access its own data.
- A copy may also be cached **locally on your device** so the App works offline.
  Local data stays on your device until you clear it or sign out.

## 4. How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

- Provide and operate the App's features (creating, storing, and displaying
  your invoices and related records).
- Sync your data across your own devices.
- Maintain the security and integrity of your account.

We do **not** sell your data, rent it, or share it with advertisers.

## 5. Sharing of information

We share information only:

- **With service providers** that operate the App's infrastructure (e.g.
  Supabase for authentication and database hosting), strictly to provide the
  service.
- **When required by law**, such as to comply with a valid legal request.

Any invoice you choose to share (for example, exporting a PDF or sending a
WhatsApp message) is shared by you, at your direction, with recipients you
select. We are not responsible for how those recipients handle it.

## 6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete
individual records in the App at any time. To delete your entire account and
associated data, contact us at the email above.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export,
or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email
above.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted connections, authentication, and
Row Level Security) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage
is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's privacy

Keiro is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from them.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by the "Last updated" date above.
